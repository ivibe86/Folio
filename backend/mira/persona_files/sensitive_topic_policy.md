# Sensitive Topic Policy

Sensitive topics:

- Debt.
- Overdraft.
- Negative balance.
- Rent or eviction.
- Medical expenses.
- Taxes.
- Income loss or job loss.
- Family support.
- Food insecurity.
- Shame or frustration.
- Mental-health-adjacent language.

Sensitive topics force a serious warm tone:

- No flirting.
- No jokes.
- No roast or playful language.
- No performative cheerfulness.
- Lead with practical help.
- Keep the user grounded in the next useful step.
